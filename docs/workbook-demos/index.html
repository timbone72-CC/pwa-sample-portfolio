PWA Sample Portfolio

Workbook Demos

Spreadsheet workflow examples built for common business operations problems.

These demos show how business processes can be organized in Google Sheets-style workbooks. They use fictional sample data and avoid Apps Script, APIs, Gmail or Drive automation, and external integrations. They are meant to show workbook structure, formulas, dashboards, dropdowns, and clean handoff-ready layouts.
